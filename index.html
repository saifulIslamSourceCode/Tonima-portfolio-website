LALON GETEE (লালনগীতি)

magazine

I am presenting this special edition dedicated to the legendary mystic, philosopher, and folk icon—Lalon Shah. His songs, ideas, and spiritual philosophy have always touched my heart and stirred my curiosity. With this publication, I wanted to craft something meaningful—a tribute that reflects not only Lalon’s life and legacy but also his deep influence on our culture, values, and way of thinking.

Lalon was more than a poet or a singer; he was a voice for the voiceless, a thinker ahead of his time, and a seeker of truth beyond boundaries. In a world still struggling with discrimination and division, his message of unity, love, and human identity feels more relevant than ever. Through Tonima, I aim to bring those messages to light—not just as history, but as a living philosophy we can still learn from.

Inside this edition, you will find thoughtful essays, original poetry, hand-drawn artwork, and in-depth reflections—all created with care and respect for Lalon’s vision. Each piece is a creative attempt to understand and express the core of Lalon’s teachings: that we are all human beyond labels, beyond religion, and beyond

This magazine is not only a creative platform, but also a personal journey for me. I’ve poured my heart into curating content that resonates with Lalon’s spirit and connects with readers of all ages. Whether you are discovering Lalon for the first time or revisiting his wisdom, I hope this edition leaves you inspired, thoughtful, and a little closer to understanding the beauty of being human.

Thank you for being part of this journey.
With sincerity and love,
-Tonima

Fashion isn’t just what we wear—it’s how we express who we are. It’s art, emotion, and storytelling woven together with threads of creativity and purpose.

As a passionate fashion designer, I don’t just follow trends—I create meaningful pieces that empower, inspire, and reflect individuality. Every design is a chance to spark confidence and turn everyday moments into runway-worthy statements. ✨

Currently, I’m proud to work as a Fashion Designer at Nafisa Int’l Trading (BD) Ltd., where I get to bring bold ideas to life, explore fresh styles, and merge innovation with modern aesthetics. My journey has also taken me to Eco Sourcing Limited, where I had the incredible opportunity to work with global giants like Inditex(Zara, Stradivarius), diving deep into the world of sustainable fashion and eco-conscious denim sourcing.

I hold a degree in Fashion Design from Shanto-Mariam University of Creative Technology, where I honed my skills in sketching (both digital and manual), trend forecasting, brand storytelling, and more. From curating mood boards to crafting detailed tech packs, I love every part of the design process—watching a simple spark of inspiration evolve into a finished piece that moves people.

But beyond the fabrics and forms, I deeply care about our planet. Nature fuels my creativity, and I believe fashion should honor it—not harm it. I’m passionate about reducing pollution, embracing eco-friendly practices, and designing with sustainability in mind—because true style doesn’t come at the cost of the Earth.

Let’s create something extraordinary—where elegance meets conscience, and fashion tells a story worth remembering. 🌿💫

🌈 Look 1: Sunset Ruffle Fantasy

This vibrant, tiered dress is a celebration of color and bold femininity. Inspired by the gradient hues of a glowing sunset, it features layers of organza in fiery orange, magenta, and deep purple, creating dynamic movement and volume. The structured bodice adds contrast with a sleek fit, enhancing the drama of the cascading ruffles below. A statement piece that’s both playful and powerful, ideal for runway or editorial settings.

🌸 Look 2: Blush Bloom Elegance

Romantic and whimsical, this blush pink gown exudes soft sophistication. The dress is adorned with floral-textured embellishments on the bodice, paired with flowing, sheer layers of fabric that create a dreamy silhouette. A touch of modern glamour is introduced through the deep neckline and shoulder details, while the delicate color palette keeps it refined. Perfect for evening events, red carpets, or couture showcases, this piece highlights delicate craftsmanship and feminine charm.
