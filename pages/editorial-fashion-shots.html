সাধনা সংগ্রাম

"The Struggle of Devotion"

The pose and attire evoke a sense of ascetic discipline and inner awakening. The script on the blouse reads “অহঙ্কার, ক্রোধ, মোহ, লোভ, হিংসা – ত্যাগ করো,” encouraging one to renounce ego, anger, attachment, greed, and envy—core tenets of spiritual struggle.

প্রেম যত্ন

"Nurturing Love"

With a warmer, sensuous silhouette and the words “ভালোবাসা মানুষে মানুষে মিলন” (Love is the union between people), this look celebrates tenderness, care, and feminine strength through simplicity and statement.

বিদ্রোহী বাউল

"The Rebel Baul"

This look blends fierce energy with rooted traditions. The bandana and stance channel rebellious spirit, and the text “আমারে কেউরে ছুঁইও না” (No one may touch me) echoes autonomy and power.

মনের রঙ

"Color of the Mind"

Graceful, poised, and introspective—this look whispers elegance. The design reflects inner richness and the vibrant spirit of the mind, especially in its bold red drape and poetic presence.
