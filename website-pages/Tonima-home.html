About

Fashion is more than just clothing—it’s a form of self-expression, a blend of creativity and craftsmanship that tells a story. As a passionate fashion designer, I bring visions to life, crafting styles that inspire confidence and individuality. Let’s create something extraordinary—one stitch at a time!

Currently, I’m working as a Fashion Designer at Nafisa Int’l Trading (BD) Ltd., where I get to experiment with innovative designs, explore new trends, and create pieces that resonate with modern aesthetics. My journey has also taken me through exciting experiences at Eco Sourcing Limited, where I collaborated with global brands like Zara and Stradivarius, focusing on sustainable fashion and eco-friendly denim sourcing.

Armed with a degree in Fashion Design from Shanto-Mariam University of Creative Technology, I have sharpened my skills in digital and manual sketching, trend forecasting, and brand analysis. From designing mood boards to developing detailed tech packs, I thrive on the entire creative process—turning ideas into beautifully crafted garments.

For me, fashion is all about blending elegance, comfort, and innovation to create something truly special. Whether designing custom pieces or contributing to large-scale collections, my goal is simple: to make fashion that speaks to the soul.

Let’s create something extraordinary—one stitch at a time!

Topic: LALON GETEE (লালনগীতি)

Download magazine

I am proud to present this special edition dedicated to the legendary mystic, philosopher, and folk icon—Lalon Shah. His songs, ideas, and spiritual philosophy have always touched my heart and stirred my curiosity. With this publication, I wanted to craft something meaningful—a tribute that reflects not only Lalon’s life and legacy but also his deep influence on our culture, values, and way of thinking.

Lalon was more than a poet or a singer; he was a voice for the voiceless, a thinker ahead of his time, and a seeker of truth beyond boundaries. In a world still struggling with discrimination and division, his message of unity, love, and human identity feels more relevant than ever. Through Tonima, I aim to bring those messages to light—not just as history, but as a living philosophy we can still learn from.

Inside this edition, you will find thoughtful essays, original poetry, hand-drawn artwork, and in-depth reflections—all created with care and respect for Lalon’s vision. Each piece is a creative attempt to understand and express the core of Lalon’s teachings: that we are all human beyond labels, beyond religion, and beyond

This magazine is not only a creative platform, but also a personal journey for me. I’ve poured my heart into curating content that resonates with Lalon’s spirit and connects with readers of all ages. Whether you are discovering Lalon for the first time or revisiting his wisdom, I hope this edition leaves you inspired, thoughtful, and a little closer to understanding the beauty of being human.
Thank you for being part of this journey.
With sincerity and love, --Tonima
